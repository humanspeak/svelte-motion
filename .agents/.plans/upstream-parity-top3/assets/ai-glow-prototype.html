Five layers, one filter, and independent orbital motion per hot-spot. Click the card (or press its button) to enter the listening state.

Layer recipe (for the svelte-motion port)

  1. color fieldA conic-gradient of the 7-color palette, angle drifting slowly — the base color travel.
  2. hot spotsFour radial-gradient blobs orbiting the perimeter at incommensurate speeds with sinusoidal wobble — this is what makes brightness travel unevenly, like the real thing. Each channel maps to a MotionValue.
  3. wavy edgeAn SVG feTurbulence → feDisplacementMap pair; two counter-scrolling copies of the same noise merged so the flow never visibly loops. Displace first, blur after — the order is what keeps the waves soft instead of crunchy.
  4. bloomThree copies of the ring at increasing band width and blur (9 / 26 / 58px), upper two on mix-blend-mode: screen.
  5. hairlineA crisp 1px inner border on the card — the contrast between sharp edge and soft glow sells the depth.
